### 7. FAQ Section (Policy-Safe Language)

**Q: What service do you provide?**
A: We help travelers prepare and submit their Vietnam entry documentation. Our team reviews your information, completes the application forms, and tracks your approval status.

**Q: Are you the official government?**
A: No. We are an independent travel assistance service. We help you navigate the process but we are not affiliated with any government agency.

**Q: Why should I use your service instead of applying directly?**
A: The official process can be confusing and time-consuming. We simplify it, check for errors, and provide support if issues arise. Many travelers prefer the peace of mind our service provides.

**Q: What's included in your fee?**
A: Our service fee covers: application review, form completion, submission, status tracking, and customer support. Government processing fees are separate and clearly shown.

**Q: Can I apply directly without using your service?**
A: Yes, absolutely. You can apply at evisa.gov.vn. We're here for travelers who prefer assistance with the process.
